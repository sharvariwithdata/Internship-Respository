1. What Is This Technology
Data Analysis is the process of collecting, cleaning, and analyzing data to find meaningful insights and support better decision-making.

2. Where It Is Used
It is used in industries like banking, healthcare, e-commerce, marketing, education, and IT companies to improve business performance.

3. Required Skillset
Important skills include Excel, SQL, Python, Microsoft Power BI, NumPy, data visualization, statistics, and problem-solving.

4. Career Opportunity
Career options include Data Analyst, Business Analyst, Data Scientist, BI Developer, Reporting Analyst, and Product Analyst.

5. Future Scope
Data Analysis has strong future demand because companies rely on data-driven decisions, AI growth is increasing, and career opportunities continue expanding worldwide.
